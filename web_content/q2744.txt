--- [The Road Less Traveled | Sister, Sister Wiki | Fandom] ---
The Road Less Traveled is the twenty-first episode of the sixth season. Plot[] The episode begins with Tia debating whether or not to take a summer internship with the WNBA in New York. When she finally makes the call, Tamera says their natural father Matt want them to spend the summer in Africa with them and that she won't be scared because they will be together. To make things worse Tyreke wanted to take Tia to his family reunion in St. Louis. Now Tia is debating between the three choices and is having weird visions as well. Meanwhile, Lisa goes wedding gown shopping.
