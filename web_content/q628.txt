[Acanthops bidens - Wikipedia]
Acanthops bidens is a species of mantis in the family Acanthopidae. This species was described by Morgan Hebard in 1922. [1] Acanthops bidens is native to Mexico. [2] ^ Hebard, Morgan (1922). "Dermaptera and Othoptera from the State of Sinaloa, Mexico". Transactions of the American Entomological Society: 157–196. Retrieved June 22, 2017.
