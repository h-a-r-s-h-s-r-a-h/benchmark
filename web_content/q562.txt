--- [12th Streamy Awards - Wikipedia] ---
The 12th Annual Streamy Awards was the twelfth installment of the Streamy Awards honoring the best in American streaming television series and their creators. The ceremony was held in The Beverly Hilton in Beverly Hills, California on December 4, 2022. It was hosted by Airrack and livestreamed exclusively to his YouTube channel, making it the first Streamy Awards to be livestreamed to a content creator's channel. It was also the first Streamy Awards to be held in-person since the 9th Streamy Awards in 2019. A number of new gaming-related awards were introduced for the 12th Streamy Awards and an award for VTubers, streamers who use a virtual avatar to present themselves. The Streamys Brand Award were featured for the fifth year in a row and the Creator Honor awards for the third.

12th Streamy Awards Overview Event Background and Purpose The Streamy Awards were established to acknowledge achievements in online video content creation, a field that gained prominence with the rise of platforms like YouTube in the mid-2000s. Founded by executive producers Drew Baldwin and Joshua Cohen of Tubefilter in partnership with digital entertainment industry figures, the awards held their inaugural ceremony in March 2009, marking the first dedicated recognition for web series and independent online producers.[6][7] This initiative addressed the absence of formal accolades for digital creators, who operated outside traditional broadcast structures but were generating substantial audience engagement and revenue through ad-supported models and sponsorships.[8] The core purpose of the Streamy Awards remains to celebrate excellence in unscripted and scripted online video across diverse formats, including vlogs, gaming streams, comedy sketches, and educational content, thereby validating the creative and entrepreneurial efforts of independent creators. By highlighting top performers in over 45 categories, the event fosters industry standards, networking opportunities, and visibility for emerging talent, while adapting to evolutions such as live streaming and short-form video.[9] This recognition draws from viewer metrics, peer nominations, and expert judging, prioritizing impact measured by views, subscriber growth, and cultural influence over conventional television metrics.[3] For the 12th edition in 2022, the awards specifically honored content produced primarily in 2021 and early 2022, emphasizing the resilience of the creator economy amid platform algorithm changes and global events like the COVID-19 pandemic that accelerated digital consumption. --- [Streamy Awards - Wikipedia] ---
The YouTube Streamy Awards, also known as the Streamy Awards or Streamys, are an awards show presented annually by Dick Clark Productions and Tubefilter to recognize excellence in online video, including directing, acting, producing, and writing. The formal ceremony at which the awards are presented takes place in Los Angeles, California. It was the first awards show dedicated entirely to web series.
