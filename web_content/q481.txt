Nepal Open University - Manbhawan, Lalitpur - 015008047 - info@nou.edu.np Nepal Open University is a prominent university in Manbhawan, Lalitpur, Nepal. It was established in 2016 AD. The government of Nepal has passed a separate act in parliament. This is the premier Open University in Nepal with equal legal status to other universities in the country. The university was set up to make higher education available to more people, especially those who haven't had the chance to attend regular colleges or universities. The university is working to create programs that are open to everyone and meet Nepal's needs by building a knowledgeable society and helping young people get ready for jobs in Nepal and around the world. The university will use the latest technology to offer these programs through online distance learning. The major feature of Nepal Open University is to offer a distance online learning mode for everyone. As per the name, this university is open to everyone interested in learning. A person who cannot attend the university regularly, s/he can join an Open University programme. The degree provided by Open University is equally valid as other universities in Nepal. It offers easy access to the study to everyone. The education program will mainly focus on science, technology, and job-related training, as well as courses that combine different subjects. These are important areas for higher education in Nepal. The university will also offer general and professional programs like humanities, social sciences, management, public administration, education, law, and other fields suggested by the university's research team. These courses will help students become skilled enough to create their jobs or find work in both the national and international job markets.
