--- [Prajakta Koli - Wikipedia] ---
Prajakta Koli (pronounced [pɾad͡ʑəkta koɭi]; born 27 June 1993) is an Indian YouTuber and actress. She is best known for her YouTube channel MostlySane and for her starring role in the Netflix romantic drama series Mismatched (since 2020) and Single Papa (2025). She has also played a supporting role in the films Jugjugg Jeeyo (2022) and Sunny Sanskari Ki Tulsi Kumari (2025).


--- [YouTube Fanfest Returns With Its 10th Year At Marina Bay Sands ...] ---
YouTube Fanfest is set to return to the live stage this year, with an offline show in Marina Bay Sands Singapore that will be livestreamed globally on 11 November 2022. YouTube Fanfest (YTFF) has been showcasing the diversity of the creator community, with regional and in-country events across APAC since its inaugural show in Singapore back in 2013. This November, YouTube will be celebrating its growth and success of YTFF, and showcasing its new wave of creator and artist talent. YouTube Fanfest 10 will be held in Singapore, in partnership with Singapore Tourism Board, Marina Bay Sands, and Singapore Airlines. At YouTube Fanfest 10, local creators Annette Lee, Zermatt Neo, Julynn Lau, Daniel Ron (Titan Gamers), will be joined alongside CarryMinati, Be YouNick from India, Nihongo Mantappu, StarBe, Ricis Official and Atta Halilintar from Indonesia, Billlie from South Korea, AC Bonifacio, Ranz, Niana and Natalia from the Philippines, Travis Japan (Johnny’s Jr. Channel), RAB リアルアキバボーイズ (Real Akiba Boyz) and Akanekikaku (アカネキカク) [Tomioka Dance Club] from Japan to perform live onstage with a high energy entertainment focused showcase from our top channels across the region. The show will be hosted by Annette Lee, Prajakta Koli aka MostlySane from India and Georgia Productions from Australia. Look forward to some music and a live appearance from some of the top Singapore creators – on stage at YTFF for the first time. Creators across multiple verticals, including Gaming, Beauty, Food and Comedy, will be sharing their content at Fanfest 10 with a series of fun cross market collaborations as we bring the best together from all over APAC – showcasing the diversity of verticals, languages, and channels in challenges, glow ups, fun food mash ups and comedy skits from the new and established YTFF stars. The event kicks off with a red carpet experience on the MBS event plaza at 5:30PM where fans will get to see the creators and artists up close and personal as they do live interviews with the hosts before the live show kicks off. Fans who want to attend the live event in Singapore will be able to sign up for General Admission (GA) tickets on PouchNation from 10:00AM on Thursday, 13 October. GA Tickets are available for free but there will be limited seating available. Superfan tickets will be available for a select group of fans as well. Superfan tickets come with front stage seating and access to a live Meet & Greet before the show. There will also be virtual Meet & Greets with creators who are sharing their content at Fanfest 10 in the week following the live show, from 17 to 21 November. Fans will be able to learn how to apply for Superfan tickets and virtual Meet & Greets through social handles of creators and artists participating in Fanfest 10. Fanfest 10 is a fully vaccinated event. All registered fans may be required to provide proof of their Covid-19 vaccination before entering the event space. The show and red carpet will be livestreamed on the YouTube Fanfest channel from 5:30PM on 11 November 2022.
