--- [Crystal Ring Shield | Dark Souls Wiki - Fandom] ---
The Crystal Ring Shield is a small shield in Dark Souls. In-Game Description - Weapon born from the mystical creature of the Darkroot Garden, the Moonlight Butterfly. - Imbued with a powerful crystal power, like the butterfly created by Seath the Scaleless. Can emit crystal light rings. Availability[] The Crystal Ring Shield can be created at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo with a +10 Shield upgraded down the Standard path, the Soul of the Moonlight Butterfly and 5,000 souls. Characteristics[] The Crystal Ring Shield is a unique small shield because its parry is replaced by an action that uses a ranged magic attack. The attack uses 4 durability points and ignores the East Wood Grain Ring. It has a very high magic defense but has the second lowest Physical defense, as well as the lowest lightning defense, of any small shield. It ties with the Effigy Shield for the highest fire defense of any small shield and has light deflection. --- [Strength - Dark Souls Wiki] ---
Strength is the stat determining one's ability to effectively use equipment with a Strength requirement or bonus. Of the starting classes, the Bandit has the highest Strength (14) and the Sorcerer and Thief have the lowest (9). Major diminishing returns for damage begin at 20 Strength, and the diminishing returns increase sharply at 40 Strength for Standard, Raw, and Crystal equipment and at 50 for Magic, Divine, Enchanted, and Occult equipment. Equipment Requirements All weapons (except the Dark Hand), shields, and spell tools have a Strength requirement, but because every class starts with at least 9 Strength roughly half of all Strength requirements are irrelevant. Lacking the Strength required to wield a weapon or shield (indicated with a red X on the D-pad menu) means the character will be unable to wield the equipment effectively: weapons will be much slower and weaker while shields will cause the wielder to stagger upon being hit and fail to block damage.
