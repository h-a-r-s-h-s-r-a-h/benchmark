Amir Talai is a voice actor who provides the voice of Alastor[1] and Tom Trench[2][3] in the Hazbin Hotel series. Career[] He is known to have voiced Crane from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness, Skidmark and Tito Lopez from Turbo Fast, and Tupp from The Lion Guard. Roles[] Hazbin Hotel[] Trivia[] - He is of Iranian descent.[4] - Amir stated in an interview that he only watched a little bit of the pilot before auditioning, and after getting the role he only watched it fully once and had not seen it again.[5] He explained that he only watched about a minute to get a vibe of the pilot and pilot voice actors, but didn't want to watch too much due to worrying that his role would be more like an impression rather than bringing himself to it.[6] - After Vivienne Medrano saw a clip on YouTube of Amir singing "You're Never Fully Dressed Without a Smile",[7] despite not knowing anything about him yet, and hoping that he was an experienced voice actor, she wanted him to voice as Alastor in Hazbin Hotel and had an audition sent to him, and in his audition he nailed the sound that she created the character to.[8][9] - According to Amir, season 1 took him a total of 18 hours to record, which happened across 6 days of 3 hour sessions.[11] - When asked if he could voice any other character in Hazbin Hotel who would it be, Amir answered Charlie, explaining that he really relates to her idealism and sweetness which he doesn't get to do very often.[12] - At the SDCC 2025 Roundtables, Amir Talai explained that after having listened to Hazbin Hotel season one and seeing the risks that some of the actors took with their voices, he realized that he had even more room than he thought and so took more risks with his voice in season two.[13] - According to the "Season 2 Q&A w/ the Cast" livestream countdown, Vivienne Medrano, Richard Horvitz, and Amir Talai made their Broadway debuts in Hazbin Hotel: Live on Broadway.[14] External Links[] References[]


--- [Alastor the Radio Demon - Wikipedia] ---
Alastor "the Radio Demon" is a fictional character created by Vivienne "VivziePop" Medrano who primarily appears in the American musical adult animated series Hazbin Hotel. He was voiced by Edward Bosco and Gabriel C. Brown in the pilot, and by Amir Talai in the animated series since 2024. A constantly-smiling sinner, Alastor returns after a seven-year absence as one of the overlords of Hell to help run a hotel known as the Hazbin Hotel, a halfway house founded by Charlie Morningstar, meant to rehabilitate the souls of those in Hell and help them earn a place in Heaven. Alastor seeks to guide and control Charlie. For a time, his soul is under the control of fellow overlord Rosie; he is later freed from her grasp. He is often accompanied by the hotel's bartender Husk (whose soul he owns) and housekeeper Niffty, and he is considered a rival by fellow overlord Vox and Charlie's father, Lucifer.
The character has received a positive critical reception, receiving particular attention as a prominent asexual character in media.
