Aug 9, 2024 · Hornet Ring is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This ring belonged to the Lord's Blade Ciaran. It boosts critical attack damage, increasing the potency of backstabs and ripostes by 30%, echoing Ciaran's lethal dagger skills against Lord Gwyn's foesPlayers can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.
