[Trevor Evans (journalist) - Wikipedia]
Sir Trevor Maldwyn Evans CBE (21 February 1902 - 10 June 1981) was a Welsh journalist and the industrial correspondent for The Daily Express. Born in Abertridwr, Glamorganshire, he started life as a miner and electrician but entered journalism after losing his job during the 1921 miner's strike.
