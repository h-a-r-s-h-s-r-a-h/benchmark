--- [Eighteenth IGNOU convocation on March 17 - Oneindia News] ---
Eighteenth IGNOU convocation on March 17 New Delhi, Mar 10 (UNI) Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) will hold its 18th convocation here on March 17. Chief Justice of India K G Balakrishna will preside over as Chief Guest and deliver the Convocation address. The Convocation will be simultaneously held at 28 regional centres of the university. More than 1.11 lakh students are eligible for receiving their degrees, diplomas and certificates during the ceremony. AS many as 11,900 students will attend the convocation at the headquarters and at other regional Centres, a IGNOU statement said. UNI


--- [IGNOU to award 3 students | Patna News - Times of India] ---
The main function will be held in New Delhi and would be relayed simultaneously at all the 28 regional centres in the country via teleconferencing. PATNA: Three students of Bihar will be awarded gold medals for securing top positions at all-India level in their respective courses at the annual convocation of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) to be held here on March 17. The toppers are Sudhir Kumar Sinha for MCA, Vijay Lakshmi for BIT and K Lochan for BSc course. The main function will be held in New Delhi and would be relayed simultaneously at all the 28 regional centres in the country via teleconferencing. Chief Justice of India K G Balakrishnan will be the chief guest in New Delhi whereas former vice chancellor of Nalanda Open University and Magadh University Shamshad Hussain will be the guest of honour in Patna. IGNOU regional director Ram Chandra said that as many as 6,192 students belonging to this regional centre will be awarded degrees, diplomas and certificates at the convocation to be held at the Bharatiya Nritya Kala Mandir. Altogether 928 students have already registered to participate in the convocation. The students have been directed to report at the regional centre at 10-30 am on March 17. The convocation will start at 2-30 pm, he added.

--- [Indira Gandhi National Open University - Wikipedia] ---
Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is a public open and distance learning university located in Delhi, India. Named after the former prime minister of India, Indira Gandhi, the university was established in 1985 with a budget of ₹20 million, after the Parliament of India passed the Indira Gandhi National Open University Act, 1985 (IGNOU Act 1985). IGNOU, operated by the Government of India is the world's largest university, with enrollment of over 3,000,000 students. It provides 333 academic programs through its 21 Schools of Studies, supported by a network of 67 Regional Centres and 2,257 Learner Support Centres (LSCs).
IGNOU has achieved an all-time high in total registrations, reaching 753,286 for the 2023–24 period (covering the admission cycle of July 2023 and January 2024). Of these, 352,354 students enrolled in bachelor's degree programs, with the Bachelor of Arts (BAG) being the most popular, garnering 109,840 registrations.
IGNOU had started a decentralisation process by setting up five zones: north, south, east, west and north-east. The Ministry of Education
has entrusted the responsibility of developing a Draft Policy on Open and Distance Learning and Online Courses to IGNOU. IGNOU also partners up with other organizations to launch courses. IGNOU offers a BBA in Retail distance learning course in association with Retailers Association of India (RAI).
