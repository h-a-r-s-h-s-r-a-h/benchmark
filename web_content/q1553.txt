The Moldovan fights for every point, and runs down practically every ball.Thanks to his years of experience as a doubles player, Albot also has an impressive game at the net. He relies on quick footwork and extensive court coverage to gain control of rallies, but can finish points with some deft volleys too.While Fritz is eight years younger than Albot and thus likely to have higher fitness levels, he might still be exhausted from the five-setter against Machac.
