Design and Display The Nokia 3208c has compact dimensions of 108.5 x 48.6 x 13.2 mm and weighs only 90 grams, making it lightweight and easy to handle. It's built with a robust plastic body available in two colors: Pearl White and Graphite Black. The phone features a 2.4-inch TFT resistive touchscreen, a choice that was part of the transitional phase from traditional keypads to touch interfaces. The display supports 256K colors with a resolution of 240 x 320 pixels, offering a modest visual experience.
