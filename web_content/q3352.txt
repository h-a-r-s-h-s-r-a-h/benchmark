--- [Weapons | Bloodstained Wiki - Fandom] ---
Weapons are in-game items that Miriam and other playable characters can equip to modify their attacks and damage outputs. Weapons only modify the Attack (ATK) stat, with the type of damage dealt either falling into the Slash , Strike , or Thrust categories, although they may also have extra elemental Attributes like Fire , Ice , Thunder , etc. Some weapons also have special abilities. Most weapons that are craftable will become available for the player to purchase at Dominique's supply post after being crafted once with Johannes. There are 10 different types of weapons: List of weapons[] This list is in the same order as the in-game codex. A select few of these weapons can be upgraded into stronger versions of themselves through the help of Johannes. Similarly, the Bloodbringer sword becomes more powerful after the Familiar Bloodbringer reaches Lv. 99, the Grand Izayoi's damage can be upgraded by absorbing blood with Blood Steal, and the Redbeast's Edge, other than through upgrading, also increases in power by eating Foods. Some of the items listed below as "Initial" start out hidden and automatically add themselves to the Alchemy Notes the moment you gather all of the required materials.
