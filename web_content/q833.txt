[Fencing 2020/Men's individual foil | Olympics Wiki | Fandom]
The men's individual foil of Fencing 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 26 July 2021 in the Makuhari Messe Hall. With 36 athletes competing, the event was won by Cheung Ka Long from Hong Kong, with Daniele Garozzo from Italy taking the silver medal and Alexander Choupenitch from Czech Republic taking bronze.

Japan has used 2 host places to complete a men's foil team (it had 1 place through individual qualifying). The COVID-19 pandemic delayed many of the events for qualifying for fencing, moving the close of the rankings period back to April 5, 2021, rather than the original April 4, 2020.
