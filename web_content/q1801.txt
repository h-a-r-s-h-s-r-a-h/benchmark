Dexter "Dexter" is a darkly compelling crime drama that follows Dexter Morgan, a forensic blood spatter analyst for the Miami Metro Police Department who leads a secret double life as a vigilante serial killer, targeting criminals who have escaped justice. Guided by a strict moral code instilled by his adoptive father, Dexter balances his day-to-day work solving crimes with his hidden, violent impulses, creating intense tension and moral ambiguity. The series explores themes of identity, morality, and the human capacity for violence, blending procedural crime-solving with deep character study, dark humor, and suspenseful storytelling. Originally airing from 2006 to 2013, it was later revived in "Dexter: New Blood" (2021) and continued in "Dexter: Resurrection" (2025). Won 2 Golden Globes.
