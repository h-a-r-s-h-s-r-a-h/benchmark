Tiki Totem From Terraria Wiki Jump to navigation Jump to search Tiki Totem The Tiki Totem is a Hardmode pet-summoning item that spawns a Tiki Spirit that floats and spins above the player's head. Unlike most pets, it will not teleport to the player when the player moves too quickly for it to keep up; it will simply float straight towards the player. The Tiki Totem can be purchased from the Witch Doctor for 2 PC when he is spoken to in the Jungle biome during Hardmode. Notes - If it is equipped alongside the Wisp in a Bottle, both pets overlap each other while the player is not moving. Trivia - The Tiki Spirit is likely a reference to the character Aku Aku from the Crash Bandicoot video game franchise. History - Desktop 1.4.0.1: Item and pet sprites updated.
