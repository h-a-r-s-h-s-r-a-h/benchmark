The women's team épée of Fencing 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 1 August 2021 in the Makuhari Messe Hall. With 8 teams competing, the event was won by the team from Estonia, with South Korea taking the silver medal and Italy taking bronze. Format[] The competition exists out of a knockout competition with each match going until the first team reaches 45 points, or after three rounds of three minutes have passed. The winner of the final wins the event. Preview[] Eight teams qualified for the event with China as the reigning world champion, though five nations had won medals at the two most recent world championships. The Chinese number one was the nation to have won two medals, taking the top spot from the United States, with ROC, Italy, and South Korea also taking medals.
