--- [Brushwood Helmet | Demon's Souls Wiki | Fandom] ---
The Brushwood Helmet is a Head Armor piece in Demon's Souls. Description[] In-Game Description - This old helmet was long ago held by the Boletarian Royal Family. It is excellent for staving off flame. Recently, Biorr of the Twin Fangs has been known for wearing it. - Its heaviness slightly impedes stamina regeneration. Availability[] - Corpse in 1-1, Pure White World Tendency required. - Dropped by Biorr of the Twin Fangs.
