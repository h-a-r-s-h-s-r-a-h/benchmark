[Italy at the 1964 Winter Olympics - Wikipedia]
Italy competed at the 1964 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, Austria. They brought 61 competitors, 53 of whom were men and 8 of whom were women. A total of 4 medals were won, including 1 silver and 3 bronze. Winners (in bold) qualified for the Group A to play for 1st-8th places.
