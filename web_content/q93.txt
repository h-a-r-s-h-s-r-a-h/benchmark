--- [Maximum Green Yellow | Crayola Wiki | Fandom] ---
The Munsell-Crayola color Maximum Green Yellow was released in 1926 as a crayon. The crayon was originally created by Munsell Color Co in 1906, before the company was bought out by Crayola and the crayon was revamped. Maximum Green Yellow was officially discontinued in 1949, although it wasn’t available from 1944 onwards from a lack of resources due to WWII.
