[We Rollin - Wikipedia]
" We Rollin " is a single by Punjabi rapper-singer Shubh. [1][2][3] The song was music composed by Anabolic Beatz, and written by Shubh, The song was released on 17 September 2021. [4][5][6] The song appeared in the UK Asian Music Chart and UK Punjabi Chart and Apple Music India Daily Chart. [7] We Rollin has Crossed 264 Million views on YouTube May 2025. [8][9][10]
