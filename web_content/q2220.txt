--- [Rhythm-and-blues star Ciara to perform at The Joy Theater on ...] ---
Rhythm-and-blues star Ciara will perform at The Joy Theater in New Orleans on May 19. Dubbed “The Jackie Tour,” Ciara’s 18-city tour is the Grammy-winner’s first in six years. Tickets go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, April 3. General admission tickets are $25, VIP reserved seating tickets are $40. Tickets and more information are available at www.wintercircleproductions.com. Ciara released her self-titled fifth album in 2013. Her breakthrough hit, “Goodies,” appeared in 2004. Atlanta-based rapper-producer Lil Jon produced “Goodies” and named Ciara the First Lady of Crunk & B.


--- [Ciara to headline the Joy Theater in May] ---
With her highly anticipated sixth studio album Jackie set for a May release, Grammy Award-winning singer Ciara will make a New Orleans tourstop on May 19. Ciara's debut, 2004's Goodies, received several Grammy nominations, including a nod for her first major hit, "1, 2 Step." Jackie follows her acclaimed 2013 self-titled album, melding breathy R&B with Janet Jackson, club music and electronic pop influences. The 2013 megahit "Body Party" is essentially a bedroom version of the eternal summer jam "My Boo." Jackie's first single is the minimal, Future-aided ballad "I Bet." The concert at The Joy Theater (1200 Canal St.) is presented by Winter Circle Productions. Tickets are $25-$40 and are on sale 10 a.m. Friday, April 3.
