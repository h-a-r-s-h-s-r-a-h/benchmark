--- [Puzzling Stone Sword | Dark Souls Wiki - Fandom] ---
The Puzzling Stone Sword is a straight sword in Dark Souls II. In-Game Description - A sword with a segmented blade. When swung, its segments separate and the sword strikes in a whipping slash. - Crafted with a rare magnetic stone of remarkable strength only found deep within caves of the Gutter. Availability[] - Found in a hidden chest in the Dragon's Sanctum. To find it, descend through a long set of stairs inside the Sanctum, jumping over a broken segment, and shoot an arrow to the switch seen high above at the far-end wall to open the chamber where the sword is found. Head back and shoot another arrow at the switch seen at the end of the window-like niche on the wall to open a nearby door. Enter this area and the chest containing the sword can be found at the bottom. - Rarely dropped by the Razorback Nightcrawlers in the corridor before the Afflicted Graverobber, Ancient Soldier Varg, and Cerah the Old Explorer boss fight. Characteristics[] - Despite its whip-like moveset, it's actually parryable. - The strong attack deals more damage if the player is close to the opponent. - Although it has low base damage, its Dexterity scaling is notorious. At Dexterity 50, the +5 variant has an attack rating of 294 (145+149). - Even after infusion, it will keep the S Dexterity scaling, making infusion a good option for Dexterity builds if the player has invested points in Intelligence or Faith. --- [Puzzling Stone Sword - DarkSouls II Wiki] ---
In-Game Description A sword with a segmented blade. When swung, its segments separate and the sword strikes in a whipping slash. Crafted with a rare magnetic stone of remarkable strength only found deep within caves of the Gutter. Availability - Dragon's Sanctum treasure. Found in a metal chest in a room unlocked by two buttons in the corridor (can only be activated with projectiles). Preceding room has wall spikes. Video Location - Razorback Nightcrawler (Cave of the Dead) drop - very rare. - Afflicted Graverobber, Ancient Soldier Varg, Cerah the Old Explorer drop - very rare (Note: Drops much more often when you are summoned to another player's world as a phantom). --- [Crown of the Sunken King Weapons - Dark Souls II Guide - IGN] ---
Checklists Crown of the Sunken King Weapons With new DLC comes new weapons, and Crown of the Sunken King, although light on new weapons, does have some fun ones that we can use. Puzzling Stone Sword Found in the Dragon Sanctum, the Puzzling Stone Sword is a very unique weapon. When being used for a light attack, you swing it like a sword, but heavy attacks cause the blade to break and the attacks mimic that of a whip. The sword requires 7 Strength and 6 Dexterity, and scales with an E in Strength and S in Dexterity, making it prime for Dex based characters. It also utilizes Twinkling Titanite to upgrade it. Sanctum Mace This large mace is a drop from the Sanctum Knights found in the Crown of the Sunken King DLC. It is fairly rare, so you will need to kill them several times to find it. It has your standard great mace attacks, however each attack will cause poison to build up in your body! The mace takes 35 Strength and 6 Dexterity to wield, with a B scaling in Strength. Looking to upgrade it? No worries, as it takes the common Titanite Shards. Yorghs Spear This is one of the two boss weapons found in the DLC. Using Sinhs Soul, you can trade it to Ornifex to obtain this weapon. It is a great spear, and uses the same moveset as any other one in the category. It takes 30 Strength and 25 Dexterity to wield, and has a C scaling in Strength and a B in Dexterity. As it is a boss weapon, it uses Petrified Dragon Bone to upgrade. Wrathful Axe This is one of two boss soul weapons found in the DLC. It's a large Halbred, created by trading the soul of Elana to Ornifex. The weapon has your standard halbred attacks, however using a L2 attack will cause it to detonate an AoE attack, centered around the player. The weapon takes 20 Strength, 22 Dexterity and 28 Faith to wield. It has a B scaling in Strength and a C in Dexterity. As it is a bos weapon, it takes Petrified Dragon Bone to upgrade it.
