--- [Istarelle | Demon's Souls Wiki | Fandom] ---
The Istarelle is a Spear in Demon's Souls. Description[] In-Game Description - A short spear of hard oak. It is covered with engravings and is one of the few Revelations from God. Once belonged to the Holy Knight Lizaia. - Its holiness increases the wielder's resistance to plague and poison. Availability[] Istarelle is found on a corpse atop one of the buildings suspended above Leechmonger's arena in 5-1. To reach it, the player must climb a ladder found outside the building where the Filthy Woman initially appears; however, the ladder only appears in Pure White World Tendency. It is guarded by several Depraved Ones as well as one giant Depraved One. Characteristics[] Like the Rune Sword, most of this weapon's damage comes from magic damage, which scales with Faith; this makes it the only spear that can scale with Faith by default. Like the Club and the Great Club, this weapon is wooden and cannot be coated in Turpentine or Black Turpentine. Checklists How creative can you get with your first free checklist? Istarelle Istarelle is a rare spear in Demon's Souls. It can be found on a secret cliffside in the Valley of Defilement (5-1), accessible via a ladder that only appears if World Tendency is Pure White. This ladder appears on a cliffside path between the hut with the plagued rats and the hut with the large depraved one. Follow the path at the top of the ladder until you reach the clearing with three more large depraved ones. Defeat them and grab the Istarelle off the ground. Istarelle's unique property is its magic damage, which scales with the Faith stat. This weapon also increases the users Poison and Plague resistance. Istarelle can be upgraded up to +5 at Blacksmith Ed using Colorless Demon Souls.
