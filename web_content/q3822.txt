Graduates are well-prepared for corporate roles, entrepreneurship, or further studies in top international universities. The BHM program prepares students for leadership roles in the hospitality and tourism industry, one of Nepal's fastest-growing sectors. Over four years, students gain expertise in hotel operations, food production, front office, housekeeping, event management, and tourism entrepreneurship.
