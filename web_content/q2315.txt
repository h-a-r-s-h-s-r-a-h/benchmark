--- [Different World (Alan Walker album) - Wikipedia] ---
Different World is the debut studio album by Norwegian DJ and record producer Alan Walker. It was released on 14 December 2018 through MER Musikk and Sony Music Entertainment and includes his successful 2015 single "Faded". The album also succeeds a trilogy of releases leading up to the album, entitled World of Walker, which consisted of the singles "All Falls Down", "Darkside" and "Diamond Heart". The album was also nominated for Top Dance/Electronic Albums at the 2020 Billboard Music Awards.


--- [Alan Walker - Different World Lyrics and Tracklist | Genius] ---
Different World is the debut album by producer-recording artist Alan Walker. The album was announced back in 2017 when “All Falls Down” was released originally as the first chapter of a concept EP, which sets out it’s own storyline. When “All Falls Down” was released, its music video was expected to form part one of a four-piece short film, which will eventually mean a four-track EP, which eventually changed over time. The trailer paints a vague picture of what can be expected from Walker’s debut album. Although the imagery throughout the video is dark, the video ends on a hopeful note of companionship among the Walkers in text messages and the eponymous topic of experiencing a different world. Furthermore, the screen text shown aligns with the moody yet hopeful vibes of the imagery, as it consists of phrases like “cause when it all falls down,” “let the darkness lead us into the light,” and “take me… dark side.”
